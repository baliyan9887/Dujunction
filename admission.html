The University of Delhi is expected to start DU 2019 admission process on the last week of May, 2019. However, St. Stephen's College will release the application forms for its UG and PG programmes on its official website www.du.ac.in soon. The Delhi University admission process for undergraduate programmes (B.A, B.Com and B.Sc etc ) will be common for all affiliated colleges except for St.Stephen's College which will be open from the last week of May, 2019. To participate in the DU admission 2019, candidates must register and fill the DU application form. DU 2019 admission will be merit-based for some programmes and through an entrance exam for some. DU Admission will be on the basis of 'Best of four' subject marks for merit-based programmes while marks scored in the entrance exam will be considered for the rest. About 9-10 cutoffs will be announced for DU admission 2019. To know more about DU admission 2019 read the article below. DU 2019 admission process is the entry to around 60,000 plus seats in the 85 colleges including affiliated and autonomous colleges of the state.

Candidates belonging to the reservation categories mentioned can apply for the same in the application form of Delhi University 2019 admission along with relevant documents to support the claim. Candidates who do not have rightful documents and do not fit the eligibility prescribed for the category concerned will not be considered for Delhi University admission 2019. The per cent of seats reserved for each of the categories is given here.

Category Percentage of seats reserved in each of the offered programmes
Other Backward Classes (OBC) 27%
Scheduled Castes 15%
Scheduled Tribes 7.50%
Person with Disabilities 5%
Kasmiri Migrants 5%
Sports/ECA quota 5%
Foreign candidates 5%
Wards/ Widow of War Army Officers 5%
