The details of the courses are mentioned below. The process of admission at Delhi University for merit-based courses is on the basis of the best of four marks of the qualifying exam.     • Eligibility Criteria for Delhi University Admission 2019

    All applicants must ensure they meet the specified DU eligibility criteria 2019.to be considered for admissions. The DU 2019 eligibility criteria details the academic qualifications, age limits, domicile conditions etc that must be met for DU 2019 admission. The details are.

  • Candidates must have completed their class 12 examination from CBSE or any other recognized state board.
  • Applicants to courses of minority institutions must also apply for DU UG admissions through the common portal.
  • The eligibility criteria of Delhi University UG admission 2019 is common for all the affiliated colleges other than St. Stephen's and Jesus and Mary Colleges.
  • University of Delhi admission 2019 has no prescribed minimum age criteria.
  • Reserved category candidates must check the special eligibility before applying for Delhi University admission 2019.

    • Documents to keep whilst filling the Delhi University 2019 Application Form

    The candidates will be needed to keep some essential documents with themselves while filling the application form of DU 2019 without which the application form will remain incomplete and the candidates will not be able to apply for DU admission 2019.

    1. Passport size photograph of the applicant (10-50 kb in size for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format).
    2. Scanned signature of the applicant (10-50 kb in size for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format).
    3. Self -Attested Class X certificate/mark sheet containing Date of Birth (100-500 kb in size for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format).
    4. Self -Attested Class XII Mark Sheet, if results have been announced. (In case the Marksheet has not been issued by the Board, a self-attested copy of the Marksheet downloaded from the respective Board’s website should be uploaded). (100-500 kb in size for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format).
    5. SC/ST/PWD/CW/KM Certificate (in the name of the Applicant) issued by the competent authority (100-500 kb in size for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format).
    6. OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate (in the name of the Applicant) issued on or after March 31, 2019. The OBC caste must be included in Central list issued by the Government of India. (100-500 kb for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format).
    7. EWS Certificate issued by SDM certifying the applicant can claim reservation under this category, dated March 31, 2019, or later. 100-500 kb in size for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format).
    8. Self-attested copies of Sports and/or ECA certificates. (100-500 kb in size per document for upload in jpg/jpeg/png format), if applying under these categories.

    How to fill Application Form 2019?

    The application form of Delhi University admission 2019 for all colleges will be available from the last week of May, 2019. However, the applications for St. Stephen's College will be released separately in the official website of college. To fill the DU 2019 application form, the candidates will have to get the access of Delhi University admission portal by getting themselves registered online. Candidates applying for undergraduate programmes must first complete the online registration process on the admission portal of the University of Delhi. The steps to fill the application form of DU admissions are -

    Step 1 - Registration:

    Candidates must first register through a one time process. This will create their user name and password to be used for future login purposes.

    Step 2 - Login and fill details:

    Candidates must enter their user name and password. They must enter their personal, bank, academic details and also the "best of four" marks. The calculation can be done using the online calculator provided by the university. The procedure to calculate the best of four is given below.

    Step 3 - Uploading documents:

    Candidates must upload the following documents

  • Passport size photograph of the applicant.
  • Scanned signature of the applicant.
  • Self Attested Class X certificate/mark sheet containing Date of Birth.
  • Self-attested Class XII Marks Sheet OR a self-attested copy of the Marks Sheet downloaded from the respective Board’s website should be uploaded in case of result not declared.
  • Self-attested copy of the valid SC/ST/OBC/PWD/KM/CW certificate, if applicable.
  • Self-attested copies of Sports/ECA certificates, if applicable

    Payment of application

    How to Calculate Best of Four for DU Admission 2019?

    Best of Four is the aggregate marks of the four subjects from the qualifying examination of the candidate where he/she has scored the best of all subjects.

    The following subjects will be included in the 'Best of four' calculation of Delhi University admission.

  • One language (Core/Elective/Functional)
  • One subject (For which candidate wished to apply)
  • Any two academic subjects

    • Candidates must note that the subjects of DU admission will differ based on the programme they wish to apply for although the language subject will remain constant.
