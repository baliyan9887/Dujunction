Delhi University 2019 Admission - Application Fee

After the candidates are done registering themselves for Delhi University admission portal and filling the application form, they can continue to remit the Delhi University admission fees online through debit cards/credit cards/netbanking. The admission fee of DU 2019 admission will vary for different categories. The table given below will give you an insight on DU admission fee.

Fee is expected to change in 2019
Category Fees (Rs.)
Registration fee for GEN/UR/OBC 150 (Non-refundable)
Registration fee for SC/ST/PWD 75 (Non-refundable)
Registration fee for Sports 100 (Additional Non-Refundable)
Registration fee for ECA 100 (Additional, Non-refundable)
